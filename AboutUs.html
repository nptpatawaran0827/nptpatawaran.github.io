Executive Summary

J×N manga café will be a locally established café located in Marikina City. Our manga cafés differ from standard coffee establishments because we can offer manga, light novels, and novels for our customers to read with the option of staying 30 minutes long or even a whole day. People can enjoy their drinks and snacks while reading a variety of books and publications in the different genres. Filling their stomachs while reading in a quiet and peaceful environment will be a good way of relaxation for them. The people will surely support our business because of the unique and new environment experience in our café. J×N Manga Café will provide a friendly, comfortable, and admirable atmosphere where the customers can read, eat, and relax at a reasonable price.

Company Profile

J×N manga café specializes in providing high-quality coffee and services to suit our clients' needs. Considering the manga and anime community in the Philippines, this kind of establishment will definitely become popular. J×N manga café will provide a wide range of reading experience for the manga and anime lovers in the society

Our Mission & Goals

The goal of J×N manga café is to create a spot where customers may unwind and enjoy themselves while sipping a drink and reading their favorite books in a calm and relaxed atmosphere. Our business provides manga books, coffees, beverages, and other connoisseurs with a wide array of choices that can satisfy their palates. We endeavor to treat our customers with respect as guests, and we plan to provide timely, efficient, courteous, and comfortable service.
